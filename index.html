ABOUT

I'm Francis, a freshman at UC Berkeley with a passion for the sights, smells, people and everything else the world around us has to offer. I have only just begun to explore the world through my love of learning languages, both human and computer.

Through human languages, I can speak to the world; through programming, I can help create the world. Human-centered design is my passion.

I am currently pursuing degrees in Business Administration and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. In my free time, I enjoy learning foreign languages, taking photos of awesome buildings, reading about human rights in the East Asian region, and traveling the world.
